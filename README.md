# IlyasTV
Online streaming TV
ilyasTV gives you a smooth, powerful, and customizable streaming experience—no external player needed and absolutely no ads. Enjoy your favorite content across multiple categories, build your own playlists, and mark your favorites with one tap.
Designed for Android Phones, Android TV, and Google TV, TVApp delivers fast performance, clean navigation, and full compatibility for big screens and mobile devices.
